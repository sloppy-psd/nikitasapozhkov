Delimobil is one of the largest carsharing companies, offering short-term vehicle rentals via a mobile app. Delimobil provides a free-floating carsharing service, allowing users to rent vehicles by the minute, hour, or day. The service is accessible through the Delimobil mobile app, where users can locate, reserve, and unlock cars without the need for physical keys.

At the heart of the identity lies the road as a metaphor for freedom and forward momentum. Blurred city lights, gradients, and horizon lines evoke the rhythm of movement and endless possibility. The redesigned "б" dynamically connects with the modified "д," emphasizing progress. Signature mint green grows more vibrant, complemented by aquamarine, navy, silver, fuchsia, and purple — each tone tuned to a different scenario.
