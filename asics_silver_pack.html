Silver Pack

The Asix Silver Pack is a unique streetwear project inspired by space and futuristic travel aesthetics. Distinctive lettering and graphics were created to reflect the essence of the Asics Silver Pack collection, evoking space orbits and futuristic interfaces. This visual identity transforms store windows into a new world, conveying the ambiance of deep space and the story of the future.
