Established in 2017, Linen Texture is a European brand specializing in linen textiles and home apparel. Operating from their production hub in Vilnius, Lithuania, the brand takes pride in overseeing every aspect of manufacturing. This hands-on approach allows for flexibility in meeting customer needs. The recently unveiled logo signifies a bold step in redefining Linen Texture's identity. Its dynamic design, reminiscent of clothing and textile patterns, captures the essence of the brand's commitment to quality and innovation. As Linen Texture continues to shape the world of linen products and home attire, the new logo and typography serves as a visual representation of their dedication to excellence.
