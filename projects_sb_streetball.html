Streetball xxl

XXL Basketball is a street sports festival hosting streetball competitions. Street Beat organized two venues, marking them with corporate colors. These venues featured quotes from great players, such as Michael Jordan and Lebron James, presented through unique typography that followed court markings. The stencil aesthetics, inspired by graffiti culture, blended seamlessly with the location, creating a distinctive, recognizable project.
