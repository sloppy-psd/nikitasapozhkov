About

Nikita Sapozhkov is a type and graphic designer whose expertise involves type development and extensive experimentation in the field. With this experience, he crafts unique wordmarks, letterings, and strong visual systems for brands, special projects, individuals, as well as cultural and art institutions.

Currently, he holds several key positions:
• Lead Type Designer at Contrast Foundry (CoFo) • Founder, Art Director and letter artist at the creative group “Any Letters Here — ALH”
