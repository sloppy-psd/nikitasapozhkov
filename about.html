Hi there! Nikita Sapozhkov is a Type & Graphic designer, also work as a Creative Director. His practice handles type design and consulting to improve typographic excellence, also covering all areas of letterworks and identities, print and interactive design for cultural institutions, businesses and individuals. Furthermore he maintains his artistic approach through sculpture and painting. He currently holds several positions: • as a lead type designer at the Contrast Foundry (CoFo) focused on retail and custom typefaces, • as a Creative Director of Moskino magazine, • and also as a Founder of the “Any Letters Here — ALH”, the creative duo founded with Egor Golovyrin. He studied the classical school of typography with Alexander Konoplev and type design in Alexander Tarbeev’s workshop. Combining these knowledges with graffiti experience, he finds his unique voice balancing between classical and underground style.
